The Solution

Paalit will provide a web-based platform where any participating specialist can post information about his or her group programs. As a program provider, you will be able to manage your group programs and collect information about them. Prospective patients can use the platform to search for appropriate programs of interest and transact on the spot.

Why groups?

Group programs such as workshops, support groups, and group therapies are excellent ways to serve more people in the same amount of time. Additionally, here are some other reasons why we think group programs are great.

  • Workshops and support groups, if framed correctly, can have less stigma associated with them
  • Many people -not only those who have a condition - can benefit from skills obtained in a workshop
  • Group therapy can actually be more effective for certain diagnoses
  • Group therapy can be a low-cost alternative for some patient segments
  • Program providers can potentially earn more per group session compared to an individual session
